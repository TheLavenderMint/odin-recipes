Chocolate Cookies

Description

This is a recipe I found online and I am very excited to try! What do you think?

Ingredients

  • 1 tin - of chickpeas, drained & rinsed
  • 3 tbsp - Oat flour
  • 2 tbsp - Peanut Butter Powder (optional)
  • 4 medjool dates
  • 2 tbsp - Cacao Powder
  • 1/2 tsp - Baking Powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • Ripe banana for extra fudgy goodness

Steps

  1. Mix everything in a food processor until well combined.
  2. Form into balls, flatten and add an optional chocolate button if you feel fancy.
  3. Into the oven at 356 fahrenheit for 15 minutes
  4. Take cookies out of the oven and enjoy!
