Healthy Chocolate Smoothie

Description

This recipe may sound weird but don't judge till you give it a try!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups - Water
  • 1 tbsp - Cacao Powder
  • 1 - Frozen Banana
  • 1/2 cup - black beans

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender. You can also add some ice if you like.
  2. Blend everything and serve.

Hope you enjoy your healthy smoothie!
