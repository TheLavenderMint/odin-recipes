Vegan Alfredo Sauce

Description

Pasta Alfredo has been my favorite meal ever since I was a little girl.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup - Cashews
  • 2 tbsp - Nutritional Yeast
  • 1 tps - onion powder
  • 1 tps - garlic powder
  • 1/2 tps - italian seasoning
  • To Taste - salt, pepper and lemon juice

Steps

  1. As you prepare the pasta, get a small saucepan and bring water to a boil.
  2. Once the water is boiling, add the cashews and leave them a few minutes to soften.
  3. Once the cashews are soft, add all ingredients to the blender with around 1 cup of water.
  4. Taste the sauce and adjust to taste, add more water if you prefer .
  5. Add the sauce to your favorite pasta.

Hope you enjoy the recipe!
